Retrieval

Three ways to get the deck out: a thumb notch in the neck's front edge (hidden under the closed lid), finger recesses through the narrow sides that dip below the stack so you can grip the cards' long edges, and a hole in the floor to push the stack up.

View & output

Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom · right-drag to pan. Both parts export in print orientation (mm, Z-up) — print base-down as shown, no supports needed. The preview and the STLs are built from the same geometry, so the print matches what you see.
